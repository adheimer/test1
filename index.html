HDS Upgrade

Coming December 14th

HDS (Hormel Decision Support) is upgrading to unlock new features and continue providing an up-to-date enterprise decision making platform.


Check it out

Features

New Look and Feel

  • Updated user interface with a more modern feel
  • Intuitive design
  • All existing data and reports
  • Single sign-on (work in progess)

New Right Click Context Menu

  • New menu with Sort, Drill, Keep Only and Remove
  • Access this by clicking on a data point, the x-axis labels, or the legend

New Subject Area Search

  • Quickly and easily find the column you are looking for
  • Create reports on the fly faster than ever

New Heat Matrix Report

  • For two-dimensional data
  • Good for analyzing large amounts of data and identifying outliers

Frequently Asked Questions

  • When will the HDS upgrade take place?

    The night of Wednesday, December 13. The system will be available the morning of Thursday, December 14.

  • Will I have access to the existing HDS after go-live?

    Yes, until Friday, January 5.

  • Will I have the same access in the new HDS?

    Yes, if you are seeing differences in your HDS access, please contact hdsteam@hormel.com.

  • When will I start receiving my scheduled reports from the new HDS?

    Starting Thursday, December 14, any scheduled reports will come from the new HDS.

  • I create and edit my own reports through Answers. Will all of these reports be available in the new HDS?

    As of November 9 a copy of all reports will be in the new HDS. Any reports changed or created after this date that are needed in the new HDS will need to be saved with special instructions - click here for instructions.

  • Will I still be able to access HDS Mobile?

    Yes, there will be a new version of the HDS Mobile app rolled out on the go-live day that will allow you to connect to the new HDS. Please allow 5 business days for this update to reach your device.

  • Will my saved customizations from my dashboards be in the new HDS?

    As of November 9 a copy of all saved customizations will be in the new HDS. Any changes after this date will need to be resaved in the new HDS after go-live.

  • Why is my homepage blank in the new HDS?

    Since this is a new system, each user's recent items will start fresh and build back up the more you use the new HDS.

  • What browser should I use?

    The HDS team recommends Google Chrome, but Internet Explorer will work as well.

  • Is there end user training for the new HDS?

    Yes - click here.
